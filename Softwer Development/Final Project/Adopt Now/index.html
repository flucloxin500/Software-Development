About

Adopt Now is a website aimed at promoting the adoption of pets who need a home to call their own. The pets available for adoption here were selected through the site The love pet project, and some of them may have already been adopted (which warms my heart). If you really want a pet, give them a chance and ADOPT, don't buy!
